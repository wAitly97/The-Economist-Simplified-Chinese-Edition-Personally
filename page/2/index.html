Chevron agreed to take over Anadarko in a $49bn deal. The acquisition expands Chevron’s shale-oil assets in America’s Permian basin, where Anadarko is a leading independent operator. It also gains a huge liquefied-natural-gas project in Mozambique. Big oil companies have been increasing their shale production targets, adding to the pressure on smaller, independent outfits to consolidate.
雪佛龙(Chevron)同意以490亿美元的价格收购阿纳达科石油公司(Anadarko)。此次收购扩大了雪佛龙在美国二叠纪盆地的页岩油资产，在那里阿纳达科是处于领导地位的独立运营者。雪佛龙也在莫桑比克(Mozambique)得到了一个巨大的液化天然气项目。大型石油公司一直在提高页岩气的产量目标，加大了规模较小的独立石油公司进行整合的压力。

Following years of costly litigation, Apple and Qualcomm settled their dispute over royalties and reached a six-year licensing agreement. Their quarrel had revolved around patent fees for the use of Qualcomm’s chips in Apple’s iPhone. While the dispute dragged on, Apple had switched to Intel to supply it with chips, but Intel had encountered problems designing chips for next-generation 5G phones and now plans to exit that business.
经过多年代价高昂的诉讼之后，苹果(Apple)和高通(Qualcomm)就专利使用费达成和解，签订了一份为期六年的授权协议。他们的争吵围绕的是高通芯片在苹果iPhone上的专利费。在这场纠纷持续的过程中，苹果转而向英特尔(Intel)寻求芯片供应，但英特尔在为下一代5G手机设计芯片时遇到了问题，现在计划退出该业务。

Notre Dame, a medieval cathedral immortalised by Victor Hugo, Hollywood and innumerable tourist selfies, caught fire. More than 400 firefighters brought the blaze under control, but the roof is gone, and with it the spire. The interior damage is extensive, but many artefacts and relics, including a supposed part of Jesus’s crown of thorns, were saved. Emmanuel Macron, the French president, visited the site and vowed that the cathedral will be rebuilt. Two French billionaires pledged a total of €300m ($340m) towards that effort.
巴黎圣母院(Notre Dame)——一座因维克多·雨果(Victor Hugo)而不朽的中世纪大教堂，也是好莱坞和无数游客自拍的圣地——遭受了火灾。400多名消防员参与扑灭火势，然而屋顶和尖顶都被烧毁了，内部也有多处遭到破坏，但是许多人工作品和文物——包括所谓的耶稣的荆棘冠冕的一部分——都保存了下来。法国总统埃马纽埃尔·马克龙(Emmanuel Macron)视察了现场，并誓言将重建它。两个法国亿万富豪承诺为这一努力提供总计3亿欧元(合3.4亿美元)的资金。

The Finns Party, an anti-immigrant outfit, won 17% of the vote in Finland’s election. Other parties do not want to work with it. The winning Social Democrats will try to form a government without it.

Saudi Aramco raised $12bn from its first bond sale on international markets. Five different bonds were reportedly sold in an offer that was heavily oversubscribed, with investors submitting $100bn in orders. Saudi’s state oil firm will put the proceeds towards its $69bn acquisition of the kingdom’s majority stake in SABIC, a chemical company, a deal orchestrated by the government. The bond sale went some way to restoring investors’ confidence in Saudi Arabia following the murder of Jamal Khashoggi, a journalist, and an opaque crackdown on top officials and businessmen.
沙特阿美(Saudi Aramco)首次在国际市场发行债券，融资120亿美元。据报道，有5种不同的债券以超额认购的形式发行，投资者提交了1000亿美元的订单。这家沙特国家石油公司将把这笔收入用于以690亿美元收购沙特化工企业SABIC多数股权，这笔交易由政府精心策划。在接连发生的对记者贾迈勒·卡舒吉(Jamal Khashoggi)的谋杀以及对政界和商界高层不透明的打击之后，此次债券发行在一定程度上恢复了投资者对沙特阿拉伯的信心。

Opening up a new front
新战线
America ratcheted up its trade dispute with the European Union, warning that it was preparing a list of tariffs on $11bn-worth of EU goods in retaliation for subsidies given to Airbus, which the World Trade Organisation has ruled are illegal. The WTO is yet to decide on the amount of tariffs that America can impose on the EU in light of that ruling, but Brussels says $11bn is an exaggeration. It is drawing up its own catalogue of American products that it will levy penalties on if Washington goes ahead with its threat.
美国与欧盟的贸易争端愈演愈烈。美国威胁会对欧盟价值110亿美元的货币加征关税，以作为欧盟为空中客车公司(Airbus)提供补贴的报复。世界贸易组织(WTO)已裁定，空中客车公司获得的补贴是非法的。世贸组织尚未决定美国可以根据该裁决对欧盟征收多少关税，但布鲁塞尔(Brussels)方面表示，110亿美元的数字有些夸张。它正在制定自己的美国产品清单，如果华盛顿(Washington)方面继续威胁，它将对这些产品征收罚款。

Following months of mass protests in Sudan, it appeared that Omar al-Bashir had been ousted as president by the army. Mr al-Bashir had misruled since taking power in a coup in 1989. His civil war against non-Muslim black Africans ended with the secession of South Sudan. Separately, the International Criminal Court charged him with overseeing genocide in Darfur.
在经历了数月的大规模抗议后，苏丹总统奥马尔·巴希尔(Omar al-Bashir)遭军方驱逐。巴希尔于1989年的政变后开始掌权。他对非穆斯林黑人发动的内战造成了南苏丹的分离。另外，国际刑事法院指控他应对达尔富尔(Darfur)发生的种族灭绝事件负责。

Binyamin Netanyahu won a record fifth term as prime minister of Israel. His Likud party tied with Blue and White, a centrist rival. But the right-wing and religious bloc, of which Likud is a part, won a majority of seats in the Knesset. In the final days of the campaign Mr Netanyahu vowed to begin annexing parts of the West Bank, further dimming the prospect of any peace with the Palestinians based on a two-state solution.
本雅明·内塔尼亚胡(Binyamin Netanyahu)创纪录地赢得了第五次以色列总理选举。他所在的利库德集团(Likud party)与中间派对手蓝白党(Blue and White)打成平手。但是利库德集团所在的右翼和宗教集团赢得了议会的多数席位。在这次竞选的最后几天，内塔尼亚胡公开宣誓要开始吞并约旦河西岸(West Bank)地区，这使在两国方案的基础上与巴勒斯坦达成和平的前景进一步黯淡。

At a product launch focused squarely on digital services (rather than a new device) Apple unveiled its video-streaming Apple TV+ app. Featuring original programmes as well as content from cable channels, such as HBO, the app will be available on certain smart televisions and on Amazon Fire and Roku. The move into Netflix’s territory comes as Apple faces slowing demand for the iPhone.

After almost two years investigating Russian interference in America’s presidential election of 2016, Robert Mueller presented his report to William Barr, the attorney-general, who released a summary.
