发表于 2019-04-18 | 分类于 《经济学人》

Following months of mass protests in Sudan, it appeared that Omar al-Bashir had been ousted as president by the army. Mr al-Bashir had misruled since taking power in a coup in 1989. His civil war against non-Muslim black Africans ended with the secession of South Sudan. Separately, the International Criminal Court charged him with overseeing genocide in Darfur.
在经历了数月的大规模抗议后，苏丹总统奥马尔·巴希尔(Omar al-Bashir)遭军方驱逐。巴希尔于1989年的政变后开始掌权。他对非穆斯林黑人发动的内战造成了南苏丹的分离。另外，国际刑事法院指控他应对达尔富尔(Darfur)发生的种族灭绝事件负责。

Binyamin Netanyahu won a record fifth term as prime minister of Israel. His Likud party tied with Blue and White, a centrist rival. But the right-wing and religious bloc, of which Likud is a part, won a majority of seats in the Knesset. In the final days of the campaign Mr Netanyahu vowed to begin annexing parts of the West Bank, further dimming the prospect of any peace with the Palestinians based on a two-state solution.
本雅明·内塔尼亚胡(Binyamin Netanyahu)创纪录地赢得了第五次以色列总理选举。他所在的利库德集团(Likud party)与中间派对手蓝白党(Blue and White)打成平手。但是利库德集团所在的右翼和宗教集团赢得了议会的多数席位。在这次竞选的最后几天，内塔尼亚胡公开宣誓要开始吞并约旦河西岸(West Bank)地区，这使在两国方案的基础上与巴勒斯坦达成和平的前景进一步黯淡。

发表于 2019-04-01 | 分类于 《经济学人》

At a product launch focused squarely on digital services (rather than a new device) Apple unveiled its video-streaming Apple TV+ app. Featuring original programmes as well as content from cable channels, such as HBO, the app will be available on certain smart televisions and on Amazon Fire and Roku. The move into Netflix’s territory comes as Apple faces slowing demand for the iPhone.
在一场明确关注数字服务（而不是新设备）的产品发布会中，苹果公司公开了它的视频数据流应用Apple TV+。这款软件以提供原创剧集和来自有线电视频道——如HBO——的内容为特色，将会在一些智能电视、亚马逊Fire和Roku中上架。这次苹果公司闯入奈飞(Netflix)领地的行动是由于其面临着iPhone需求放缓的问题。

Purdue Pharma, which makes OxyContin, an opioid painkiller blamed for a surge in addiction and overdose deaths in America, paid $270m to settle a civil lawsuit brought by the state of Oklahoma. Dozens of lawsuits have been lodged against Purdue and other drug companies in America. Oklahoma claimed that Purdue’s aggressive marketing of OxyContin drove the epidemic of opioid addiction. Charitable trusts funded by the Sackler family, which owns Purdue, are on the defensive; several museums say they will not accept further donations.
普渡制药公司(Purdue Pharma)支付了2.7亿美元，以了结俄克拉荷马州提起的民事诉讼。其生产的奥斯康定(OxyContin)是一种阿片类止痛药，后者被指导致美国吸毒成瘾和过量死亡人数激增。普渡公司和美国其他药品公司已被提起数十起诉讼。俄克拉荷马州宣称，普渡公司对奥斯康定激进的宣传导致了阿片类药物成瘾的流行。由拥有普渡公司的萨克勒家族提供资金的慈善信托基金十分被动；几家博物馆也表示他们将不会接受进一步的捐款。

After almost two years investigating Russian interference in America’s presidential election of 2016, Robert Mueller presented his report to William Barr, the attorney-general, who released a summary. The special counsel found no collusion between anyone on Donald Trump’s campaign and the Russians who had meddled in the election. Questions about whether the president tried to obstruct justice were left “unresolved”. Democrats were not pleased; they want Mr Barr to release the full report to Congress.
在对俄罗斯干预美国2016年总统选举这一事件的调查进行了两年之后，罗伯特·米勒(Robert Mueller)向司法部长威廉·巴尔(William Barr)提交了报告，后者发布了一份摘要。特别委员会没有发现唐纳德·特朗普(Donald Trump)的竞选与曾干预选举的俄罗斯人有瓜葛。而关于总统是否试图阻碍司法公正的问题“尚未解决”。民主党人并不满意；他们想要巴尔向国会提交整个报告内容。

In a sharp reversal of its earlier position, the Justice Department said it would now support striking down the whole of Obamacare, rather than certain aspects of it. The health-care act is going through a tortuous legal appeals process and will probably end up before the Supreme Court.
与之前的立场截然相反，司法部门表示他们如今会支持驳回整个奥巴马医改的内容，而不是只是其中某些方面。这份医保法案正在经历一个曲折的法律上诉过程，并且很可能会最终被提交给最高法院。
